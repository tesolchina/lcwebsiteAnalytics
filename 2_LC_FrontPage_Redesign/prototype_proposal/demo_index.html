A Message from Our Director

Cissy Li, Head of Language Centre

Welcome to the Language Centre at Hong Kong Baptist University. For over two decades, we have been dedicated to fostering linguistic excellence and cultural understanding in our diverse academic community.

Our mission extends beyond language instruction—we create transformative learning experiences that empower students to communicate confidently across cultures and succeed in an interconnected world.

Whether you're beginning your language journey or seeking advanced proficiency, our experienced faculty and innovative programs are here to support your academic and personal growth.

Join us in celebrating the power of language to bridge cultures and open new possibilities.
