Competition Guidelines

General Rules

  • Eligibility: Open to all registered Language Centre students
  • Originality: All submissions must be original work
  • Language Requirements: Varies by competition
  • Submission Format: Follow specific guidelines for each competition
  • Judging Criteria: Detailed rubrics available for each competition

Disqualification Conditions

  • Plagiarism or non-original content
  • Late submission beyond deadline
  • Failure to meet format requirements
  • Inappropriate or offensive content
