Submission Guidelines

How to Submit Your Work

We welcome original submissions from all Language Centre students:

Submission Requirements

  • Originality: All work must be original and unpublished
  • Length: 500-5000 words (varies by category)
  • Format: Submit in .doc, .docx, or .pdf format
  • Language: English, Chinese, or other languages as appropriate
  • Attribution: Include author name, course, and contact information

Selection Process

  1. Initial review by faculty committee
  2. Peer review and feedback
  3. Final selection for publication
  4. Editorial review and preparation
  5. Publication and promotion

Recognition & Opportunities

Benefits of Publication

  • Academic Portfolio: Enhance your academic and professional portfolio
  • Recognition: Author recognition at annual awards ceremony
  • Networking: Connect with faculty and professional mentors
  • Skills Development: Gain experience in academic publishing
  • Future Opportunities: Potential for external publication recommendations
