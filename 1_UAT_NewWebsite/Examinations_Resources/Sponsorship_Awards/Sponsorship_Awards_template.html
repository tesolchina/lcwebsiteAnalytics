Sponsorship and Awards

Planning Note: This section consolidates all scholarship, grant, and award information.

Recognize excellence and support your language learning journey through our various sponsorship programs and awards.

Important: Application deadlines vary by program. Please check individual award requirements for specific dates.

Academic Excellence Awards

Up to HK$5,000

Recognition for outstanding academic performance in language courses.

  • Dean's List recognition
  • Course-specific awards
  • Semester honors
  • Graduation awards

Eligibility: Enrolled students with exceptional grades

Language Proficiency Scholarships

HK$2,000 - HK$8,000

Financial support for students demonstrating exceptional language proficiency.

  • IELTS achievement awards
  • TOEFL excellence grants
  • HSK proficiency scholarships
  • Putonghua certification awards

Eligibility: Students achieving specific proficiency benchmarks

Community Service Awards

HK$1,000 - HK$3,000

Recognition for students who contribute to the language learning community.

  • Peer tutoring recognition
  • Volunteer service awards
  • Community outreach grants
  • Leadership development funds

Eligibility: Students actively involved in community service

Research and Innovation Grants

HK$3,000 - HK$10,000

Support for students conducting language-related research projects.

  • Independent research funding
  • Conference presentation support
  • Publication assistance
  • Innovation project grants

Eligibility: Students with approved research proposals

Application Process

How to Apply

  1. Review eligibility requirements for each award
  2. Prepare required documentation
  3. Submit application by the specified deadline
  4. Await notification of results

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Academic transcripts
  • Personal statement
  • Letters of recommendation
  • Supporting evidence (certificates, portfolios, etc.)
